LIST OF ALTERNATIVE LEARNING PROGRAMS

Alternative Learning Programs are extra-curricular programs that scholars from Grades 7 to 10 (FAYP) can participate. Each program has their own covered fields and themes that can help scholars find their passion.

Core Subject: English/Filipino

A club for reading and literature!
